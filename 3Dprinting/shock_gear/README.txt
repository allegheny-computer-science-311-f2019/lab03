# Summary

This is a landing gear for the Bebop 2.  using the stock mounting screws.  Use 1/16 in shock cord or light extension springs to give about a 2 inch lift for deep grass and softer landings.  Use small wood screws to mount the legs to the extensions.  Screws hold legs in place and act as pivot point.  video of action : https://youtu.be/3bs2jehNKvs
Import legs in mm, gear plates in inches

# Print Settings

Printer: da Vinci 1.1Plus
Rafts: No
Supports: Yes
Resolution: .2 or .1
Infill: 50% or more

Notes: 
***********Import legs in mm, gear plates in inches*********
Shells thick for rigidity.  print 4 legs and 2 each of the gear plates. print legs laying horizontal similar to the picture of the leg, with the round peg to the print bed, and supports along the long axis.

# How I Designed This

## designed to replace stock bebop 2 landing gear with stock mounting screws.

remove stock landing gears, assemble leg on gears, use shock cord or small light weight extension springs to as needed to get even leg positions.
